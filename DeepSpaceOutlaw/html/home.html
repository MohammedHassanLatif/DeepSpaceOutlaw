A Game by Hassan Latif and Fraser McAulay 2020-2021 students

You're a wanted man!

Deep Space Outlaw is a 2D top-down shooter where the player must take control of Norman Bates, an outlaw wanted by the intergalactic government for numerous crimes. The main objective is to fight off as many of their ships as you can.

They may have you surrounded, but you can still go out in a blaze of glory!

Will you go for the high score?
